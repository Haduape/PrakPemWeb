Kessoku Band is the fictional band featured in the anime and manga series Bocchi the Rock! The band plays a central role in the story, as it is where the main character, Hitori Gotou   (nicknamed "Bocchi") , joins to pursue her dream of becoming a rock musician despite her intense social anxiety.

The name "Kessoku Band" is a play on words, as "Kessoku" means "unity" or "binding together" in Japanese, which reflects the idea of teamwork and bonding in the band, while it can also hint at "tying" or "pressure," playing into Bocchi's nervous nature.
